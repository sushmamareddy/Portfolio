Implemented a gating network within the MoE model to select from multiple expert networks focusing on semantic similarity, contextual relevance, and syntactic coherence.

Achieved significant performance improvements, with the MoE model reaching an accuracy of 0.73 and an F1 score of 0.72, compared to BERT's accuracy of 0.6 and F1 score of 0.53, using datasets like the Story Cloze Test.
